# Main Results

## 1. Bundle-Level Formulation

The thesis develops a coordinate-free formulation of grand unification on curved spacetime using principal bundles and associated bundles.

## 2. Lorentz and Spin(10) Structures

The construction treats the Lorentz spin structure and the internal Spin(10) gauge structure as distinct but parallel geometric structures over the same spacetime base.

## 3. Associated Spinor Bundle

Matter fields are represented as sections of associated spinor bundles carrying both Lorentz spinor structure and Spin(10) representation content.

## 4. Unified Covariant Derivative

A unified covariant derivative is formulated so that the spin connection and Spin(10) gauge connection act together on the relevant spinor fields.

## 5. Clifford-Algebra Framework

Clifford algebras provide the algebraic language for gamma matrices, spinors, chirality, charge conjugation, and the construction of spin groups.

## 6. Standard Model Embedding

The thesis studies how Standard Model representations arise within SU(5) and Spin(10) grand-unified structures.

## 7. Anomaly and Consistency Checks

Anomaly cancellation and hypercharge quantization are discussed in the context of grand-unified embeddings.

## 8. Curved-Spacetime Action

The thesis assembles a curved-spacetime action involving gravity, Yang-Mills gauge fields, fermions, Higgs sectors, and Yukawa couplings in the Spin(10) framework.

## 9. Topological Sectors

The thesis discusses topological objects such as monopoles, instantons, Chern-Simons forms, and characteristic classes in the bundle-theoretic language.

## 10. Conceptual Contribution

The main conceptual contribution is a unified computational language for treating spacetime spin geometry, gauge theory, Clifford algebra, and grand-unified representation theory.
