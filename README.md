# Clifford Algebra and Principal Bundles for Spin(10) Grand Unification

## Overview

This repository summarizes my MSc thesis in Physics at Heidelberg University:

Clifford Algebra and Principal Bundle: Toward Grand Unification by spin(1,3) and spin(10)

The thesis develops an algebraic-geometric framework for grand unification on curved spacetime using Clifford algebras, spin groups, principal bundles, associated spinor bundles, covariant derivatives, curvature, and representation theory.

The central idea is to treat spacetime spin geometry and internal grand-unified gauge symmetry in a common geometric language. In this formulation, Spin(1,3) describes the local Lorentz spin structure, while Spin(10) describes the internal grand-unified gauge symmetry.

## Main Themes

- Clifford algebras and spin representations
- Pin and Spin groups
- Spin(1,3) and Lorentz spinors
- Spin(10) grand unification
- Principal bundles and associated vector bundles
- Connections, curvature, and covariant derivatives
- Gauge theory and gravity in bundle language
- Standard Model representations
- SU(5) and Spin(10) unification
- Symmetry breaking chains
- Anomaly cancellation and hypercharge quantization
- Curved-spacetime Lagrangian formulation
